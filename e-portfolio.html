e-Portfolio

Introduction

The E-Portfolio is a culminating project required for the completion of a
Master of Library and Information Science degree at Wayne State University.
This portfolio includes a reflective essay that includes two Learning
Outcomes, one optional and the other required. These Learning Outcomes are
statements of what students will have achieved upon completion of the program.
The E-Porfolio will also include

Learning Outcomes

  1. Criticaly evaluate, synthesize, and disseminate information.
  2. Understand complex interactions between diverse users, societal factors,
    and information environments affect professional situations.
  3. Assess, adopt, and utilize the most relevant information technologies.
  4. Utilize current management and leadership theories and practices in
    the workplace.
  5. Evalaute and apply library and information science research to problems
    of professional practice by employing theories, best practices, and
    assessment strategies to the range of the information functions.
  6. Practice professional engagement through leadership, service work,
    lifelong learning and community involvement.

Reflective Essay

The required Learning Outcome for the reflective essay is: Criticaly evaluate,
synthesize, and disseminate information. The optional Learning Outcome I selected for the reflective essay is: Assess,
adopt, and utilize the most relevant information technologies.
